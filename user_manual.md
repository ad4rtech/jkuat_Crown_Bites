# 📖 Crown Bites ROKMS — User Manual

Welcome to the **Crown Bites Restaurant Operations & Kitchen Management System (ROKMS)**. This manual is designed to help you navigate your specific role within the app, whether you are serving guests, cooking meals, managing payments, or overseeing the restaurant.

---

## 🔐 1. Getting Started: Logging In

When you open the Crown Bites app on your tablet, you will be greeted by the Splash Screen followed by the secure **PIN Login Pad**.

1. **Enter your 4-digit PIN**.
2. The system will securely verify your identity.
3. You will automatically be routed to the correct dashboard based on your assigned role (Waiter, Kitchen, Cashier, or Manager).

---

## 🍽️ 2. Waiter Guide (Front of House)

As a Waiter, your primary goal is to seat guests, take accurate orders, and ensure food is served hot and fast.

### Assigning a Table
1. Tap the **Select Table** (Armchair icon) from your dashboard.
2. You will see a visual map of the Main Dining area and Outdoor Patio. 
   - **Green** = Available
   - **Red** = Occupied/Eating
   - **Yellow** = Ordered (Waiting for Kitchen)
3. Tap an available **Green** table.
4. A bottom menu will slide up. Tap **Assign Table** to claim it for your guests.

### Taking an Order
1. Tap a table you have assigned, then tap **Make Order**.
2. Browse the digital menu by swiping through categories (e.g., Starters, Mains, Drinks).
3. Tap the **+** button next to an item to add it to the cart.
4. Tap the **Cart** icon at the bottom to review the order.
5. Tap **Submit Order**. The kitchen will instantly receive the ticket.

### Serving Food & The "Eating" State
1. When the kitchen marks food as ready, you will receive a **Notification** (bell icon will show a red dot).
2. Go to the **Active Orders** (Waiters Dashboard).
3. Find the order marked **Ready** (Green badge) and deliver the food to the table.
4. Tap **Mark as Served**. 
5. The order will now move to the **Eating** tab, and the table will stay occupied while the guests eat.

### Requesting Payment & Freeing a Table
When the guests are ready to pay:
1. Tap the table and tap **Make Order** to view the bill (or go to the **Billing** screen).
2. **M-Pesa STK Push**: Enter the customer's phone number and tap send. Their phone will prompt them for their PIN.
3. **Cash**: Tap "Request Cashier" to alert the cashier desk.
4. Once the bill is successfully paid, go back to the **Active Orders -> Eating** tab.
5. The gray "Awaiting Payment" button will turn red and say **Free Table**. 
6. Tap **Free Table** to reset the table to green (Available) for the next guests.

## 👨‍🍳 3. Kitchen Staff Guide (Back of House)

As Kitchen Staff, your screen is designed to be completely hands-free until an action is needed. Your goal is to cook tickets in the correct order.

### The Active Queue
- New orders automatically appear in the **Active Queue**.
- The system automatically sorts tickets by urgency:
  - **Green Timer**: Fresh order (0-9 minutes).
  - **Yellow Timer**: Warning (10-19 minutes).
  - **Red Flashing Timer**: Critical / Overdue (20+ minutes).

### Managing Tickets
1. **Start Cooking**: When you start preparing a ticket, tap **In Prep**. The color changes to orange, letting the waiter know food is being made.
2. **Order Complete**: When the food is plated and ready to go to the table, tap **Mark Ready**.
3. The ticket moves to the **Ready for Pickup** section, and the Waiter gets a ping on their tablet instantly.

### Order Collected
- Once the Waiter picks up the food and serves it, the ticket will automatically disappear from your screen and move into the **Collected Orders** tab at the bottom for your records.

## 💰 4. Cashier Guide (Billing & Reports)

As a Cashier, your responsibility is to ensure all bills are settled accurately and to manage the daily cash float.

### Processing Payments
1. Navigate to the **Billing Dashboard**. You will see a list of all active tables that have not yet paid.
2. If a Waiter requested cash, the table will be highlighted blue and say **Awaiting Cashier**.
3. Tap the table to open the final receipt.
4. **Paybill**: If the customer paid via Paybill, verify the code and tap "Mark as Paid via Paybill".
5. **Cash**: 
   - Tap "Pay with Cash".
   - Enter the exact **Cash Tendered** (what the customer handed you).
   - If the amount is less than the bill, the system will alert you of **Insufficient Funds**.
   - If the amount is more, the system will tell you exactly how much **Return Change** to hand back.

### Shift Revenue Dashboard
1. Go to the **Shift Reports** tab.
2. You can view your **Total Cash Payments** and **M-Pesa Payments** for the day.
3. The system tracks the **Change Dispensed** automatically.
4. At the end of the day, check the **Expected Cash in Till** block. This number is exactly what you should have in your drawer (Starting Float + Cash Revenue - Change Dispensed).

## 📊 5. Manager Guide (Admin)

As a Manager, you oversee the menu catalog and high-level operations.

### Menu Management
1. Open the sidebar menu and select **Manager Menu**.
2. **Add Items**: Tap the **+** icon to create a new menu item, set its price, and assign it to a category.
3. **Toggle Availability**: If the kitchen runs out of ingredients for an item (e.g., Salmon), simply toggle the switch next to it. 
4. It will instantly disappear from the Waiters' tablets so they cannot order it by mistake.

### Monitoring Notifications
- Managers receive all critical system alerts. Check the **Notifications** bell icon to monitor if tables are requesting cashiers or if the kitchen is falling behind on overdue tickets.

**Need Help?**
